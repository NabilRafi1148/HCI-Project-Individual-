Sunbic Online University

Sunbic provides distance learning programs for Diploma and High School graduates who want to pursue
their ambitions of acquiring a bachelor's degree (S1), with extremely flexible lecture schedules that allow
students to do other things without worrying about interrupting their studies.

Study Program

The following are the study programs that we offer.

Accounting

This program is for students interested in becoming professional accountants. This program's curriculum follows worldwide accounting education trends, such as the International Financial Reporting Standard (IFRS), which became the global standard for all corporations in 2012.

Business Management

This program prepares students for professions in business and to become entrepreneurs with managerial skills. With a high-quality curriculum that allows students to improve their academic abilities in accordance with their capabilities and interests in the field of management and business.

Graphic Design

This program prepares students to contribute to nation building by providing local and global knowledge of creative visual communication, as well as history and local values, technology, and entrepreneurship, so that they can work in the creative industry and be accepted through the application of visual communication design science.

Industrial Engineering

Students in this program will learn about general engineering, core industrial engineering, fundamental science, social science and psychology, computer management science, basic laboratory science, professional engineering practice, and engineering specialty industry.

Information System

This program aims to educate students with the fundamental skills and knowledge needed to work as a systems analyst in the development of information systems. Students will gain knowledge and information systems, which will be useful in the study of strategic information systems, e-business, and business intelligence.

Informatic Engineering

This program teaches fundamental and advanced computer science topics such as algorithms, application development strategies, and database technologies, as well as mathematical ideas and knowledge. The curriculum is based on the international ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) curriculum as well as input from business and industry.
